Beta Telemetry
CW CAT sends data every 10 minutes to help improve decoding. Here's exactly what we collect:
Your callsign — identifies your station
Decoded spots — callsign, frequency, SNR, WPM, decoded CW text
Channel stats — how many signals were detected, evicted, peak count
Packet stats — IQ packets received and dropped
App version & OS — for compatibility tracking
Never collected: IQ audio, radio settings, passwords, or IP addresses.
